How Blood Bridge Works

Simple, transparent, and life-saving — from registration to saving a life in three easy steps.

01
📝

Register as Donor

Fill your blood group, city, and contact details. Your data is secure and only shared when there's an urgent match in your area.

02
🔔

Get Matched & Notified

When someone nearby needs your blood group, you'll be notified on WhatsApp. You confirm availability at your convenience.

03
🏥

Donate & Save a Life

Visit the blood bank or hospital, donate safely. You can donate every 3 months. A certificate is provided for every donation.

India's Most Trusted Blood Donor Platform

Blood Bridge was founded in 2019 by a group of IIT Bombay alumni who lost a family member due to blood unavailability. Determined to bridge this gap, they built India's first community-driven blood donor network.

We operate across 28 Indian cities, connecting verified blood donors with patients, hospitals, and blood banks. Our network is completely free — no commissions, no charges, no middlemen.

We are registered under the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Government of India, and comply with all National Blood Transfusion Council (NBTC) guidelines.
